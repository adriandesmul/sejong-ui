2019 Required Repertoire

All pieces are provided as downloadable PDF files.

The 2019 Music Competition will be using an updated list of repertoire pieces from previous years. Individual division repertoire collections are $5 each. Full violin or piano repertoire collections are additionally available for $10. Violin pieces include solo part and score.

Please note that students must play the required piece appropriate for the division in which s/he will compete.

Purchases may be made through PayPal (links provided below). Once you have made a purchase, an email will automatically be sent to you with a download link for your music. If you do not receive a download link within fifteen minutes, please email us at sejong@sejongculturalsociety.org.

If you would like to purchase repertoire for a future competition, please email sejong@sejongculturalsociety.org - DO NOT purchase the previous year's repertoire.
